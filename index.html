🏔️ OpenNekaise

Buildings consume 40% of the world's energy. They are the largest, most overlooked machines on Earth. We're changing that.

Every day, they generate mountains of data — sensors, meters, controllers, alarms — an endless stream of signals. But data without meaning is just noise.

We refuse to accept that. We're building OpenNekaise — and shipping Nekaise Agent to every building. It senses the data, reasons about it, and acts on it — proactively, before problems become failures.

24/7, online, in your Slack, your Teams, or wherever your team already works — like a building engineer who never clocks out.

Open source. Open to all. Because the built environment belongs to everyone, and so should the intelligence to operate it.
