🏔️ OpenNekaise

Buildings consume 40% of the world's energy. Every day, they generate mountains of data: sensors, meters, controllers, alarms, an endless stream of signals. Most of it ignored.

What if buildings could sense that data, reason about it, and act on it, proactively, before problems become failures?

Under TwinVista, a large research project at KTH, we picked this work and open sourced it to the public.

We're building OpenNekaise, and shipping Nekaise Agent to every building.

An AI Agent that lives in your Slack, your Teams, or wherever your team already works.

An employee who never clocks out.

Open source, built with an open community.

Because building intelligence can unlock a better future for humanity.
